Waterfall vs Agile
Block 3 · SDLC
15 / 54
Two Ways to Build
How teams
organize work.
Waterfall
Structured & Sequential
Complete each phase fully before moving to the next. Very structured and documented. Used in large companies, especially in Japan. → Straight staircase, one direction.
Agile
Flexible & Iterative
Build in small rounds called "sprints." Flexible — adapt as you go. Used by most startups. → Circular loop, constantly revisiting.

Build until tests pass. Your QA Tester role today is the human version of this. Industry standard at mature engineering teams.
